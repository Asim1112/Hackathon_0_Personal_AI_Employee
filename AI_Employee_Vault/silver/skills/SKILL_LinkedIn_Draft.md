# SKILL: LinkedIn Draft (Silver Tier)

## What This Skill Does

Auto-drafts professional LinkedIn sales/promotional posts based on business events,
opportunities, or manual prompts. Every draft is written to `Pending_Approval/` and
requires human approval before the LinkedIn MCP posts it.

**Core principle:** Claude drafts, human approves, LinkedIn MCP posts.

---

## When to Run This Skill

Run this skill when:
- A `type: linkedin_opportunity` file appears in `Needs_Action/`
- The user says "draft a LinkedIn post about X"
- A business milestone, new service, or client win warrants promotion
- The daily 8 AM briefing identifies a promotional opportunity

---

## Pre-Conditions (check before starting)

- [ ] Read `Company_Handbook.md` Section 12 (LinkedIn Content Strategy) in full
- [ ] Check the LinkedIn Queue in `Dashboard.md` â€” max 1 post per day in queue
- [ ] Verify the topic is NOT on the prohibited list (client names, financials, complaints)

### Step 1 â€” Identify the post topic

## Prohibited Content Checklist

Before finalising any draft, verify these are all clear:

- [ ] No client names (unless they have given explicit written consent)
- [ ] No financial figures (revenue, invoice amounts, salary, profit)
- [ ] No confidential project details or proprietary methodology
- [ ] No negative sentiment (complaints, disputes, competitor criticism)
- [ ] No unverified claims (awards, statistics without sources)
- [ ] No promises of specific outcomes to readers
