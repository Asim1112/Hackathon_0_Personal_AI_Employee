Running a professional services business means managing a constant stream of communications across multiple channels — client emails, LinkedIn messages, social content, and financial administration — all simultaneously, often all before 10am.

The Gold Tier Personal AI Employee is built to handle that operational overhead. The system monitors five channels in parallel: Gmail, LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, and Odoo ERP.
