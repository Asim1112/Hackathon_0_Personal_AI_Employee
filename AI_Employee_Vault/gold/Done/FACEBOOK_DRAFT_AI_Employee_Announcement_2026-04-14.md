The Gold Tier Personal AI Employee monitors five live business channels at once — Email, LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, and Odoo ERP — and handles triage, drafting, and scheduling across all of them. For professional services businesses and consultancies, that means every inbound message gets classified, every reply gets drafted, and every outbound action gets prepared without staff having to manually process each one.

The critical design principle is approval before execution. Every email send, social media post, invoice creation, and payment action sits in a review queue until a human explicitly approves it. The system does not post to your LinkedIn feed, send an email to a client, or record an Odoo invoice entry without sign-off. That is not a workaround — it is the architecture. It is what makes AI automation viable in environments where your clients, auditors, or partners expect human accountability.

Every action the system takes is logged. Timestamp, actor, target, outcome — written to a structured audit file at the close of each processing cycle. If a question arises about what was sent, posted, or created and when, the answer is in the log.

This was built for operations managers and business owners who need their processes to run faster but cannot afford to lose oversight. If that describes your situation — or if you have been watching AI automation from the sidelines because the control question was never satisfactorily answered — what would it take to make a version of this work in your business?
